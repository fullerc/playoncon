Play On Con

Companion app for Play On Con, a tabletop and gaming convention at the Alabama 4-H Center in Columbiana, AL.

Privacy

Play On Con is built to work offline and does not have a backend server. Here is exactly what the app does and does not do with your data.

What the app stores on your device

  • The convention schedule, cached locally so the app works without a network connection.
  • Your “My Schedule” selections and reminder preferences for each saved event.
  • Your last custom reminder time, so the picker remembers your preference.

This data lives only on your device. It is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared. Uninstalling the app deletes it.

What the app sends over the network

  • A request to a public Google Sheets CSV URL to download the latest schedule.
  • If you tap the Discord link, your device opens the Discord invite URL in a browser or the Discord app.

No personal data is sent in either case. The app does not contact any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking service.

Location

The app can show a “you are here” dot on the venue map. Location access is opt-in: it is off by default, and the app only requests permission when you tap the locate button on the map. Your location is used solely to draw the dot on the map and is never transmitted off your device or stored.

Notifications

If you set a reminder for a saved event, the app schedules a local notification on your device. Reminders are delivered by your device’s operating system. No notification server or push service is involved.

Accounts and ads

There are no accounts, no logins, no advertising, and no in-app purchases.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. It does not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else, because it does not collect information at all.
